About

Betul Erenel is a web developer based in the Bay Area of California. With a bachelor's degree in Environmental Engineering and seven years of experience in that field. However, her passion for web development led her to pursue a career change, and she is currently a student in the Web Developer Certificate Program at DeAnza College. In addition, she is completing the MIT xPRO Professional Certificate in Coding: Full Stack Development program.
Betul has learned the importance of hard work, efficiency, and communication throughout her career. These values are evident in her dedication to creating high-quality web development projects for her clients. In her free time, Betul enjoys staying up-to-date on the latest web development technologies and best practices and always looking for new ways to improve her skills. She is excited to share her work and skills with you and hopes you find something that interests you in her portfolio. Thank you for visiting!
